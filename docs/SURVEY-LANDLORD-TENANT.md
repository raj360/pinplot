# PlotPin — Landlord & tenant survey

**Purpose:** Validate free listing + paid unlock before scaling ads.  
**Target:** 15–25 landlords (Kampala + diaspora corridor), 30–50 tenants.  
**Tool:** Google Form / Typeform · 5–8 minutes each.

---

## Landlord survey

### Screening

1. How do you currently find tenants? (FB / Jiji / agent / word of mouth / other)  
2. Listed on PlotPin or similar map app? (Y/N)  

### Pain & behavior

3. Biggest frustration when listing property? (open)  
4. Average days to fill a vacancy? (<7 / 7–30 / 30–90 / 90+)  
5. Would you pay for **serious inquiries only** vs free listings with spam? (1–5)  

6. When would you willingly pay? (multi-select)  
   - One-time verify badge on map  
   - Featured/boost slot  
   - When tenant actually rents (success fee)  
   - Monthly subscription  
   - **Never — only free**  

7. Max fee for **2-week featured boost** (UGX bands: 0 / 25k / 50k / 100k / 100k+)  
8. What makes you mark a unit **rented** promptly? (reminders / easy button / nothing)  

### PlotPin concept

9. “Tenants pay ~20k to unlock your contact for 72h; first payer gets exclusivity.” Fair? (1–5 + why)  
10. “Listing on map is **free** after we verify you.” Would you use PlotPin over Jiji? (1–5)  
11. Preferred model: (radio)  
    - **A)** Free list + optional boost later  
    - B) Small one-time verify + optional boost  
    - C) Pay every time unit goes live  
    - D) Pay only when tenant unlocks  

12. One dashboard feature you’d open weekly? (open)  

---

## Tenant survey

1. Where do you search for rentals?  
2. Paid **200k+ UGX to a “blocker”** for tours without meeting landlord? (Y/N)  
3. Would you pay PlotPin to unlock **landlord direct** + exact location? (Y/N/Maybe)  
4. Max per unlock (UGX / USD bands)  
5. Is **72h exclusive access** good or bad? (why)  
6. What makes you distrust a listing? (multi: no photos / vague area / stale / scam)  
7. Diaspora: search for family back home? Pay by card in USD? (Y/N)
